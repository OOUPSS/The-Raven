About the Project

303rd year of the Era of Magic's Disappearance on the planet Corvus.
Once, magic was an inseparable part of life. It flowed through the veins of this world, supporting its harmony and developing everything around. Great wizards ruled, miracles were everyday occurrences, and ancient spells left traces in every corner of Corvus. But over time, magic began to fade. At first, it was barely noticeable: small disruptions, weakening powerful spells, the disappearance of rare creatures. Then the wizards themselves disappeared, and powerful artifacts began to dull and lose their power.

Now magic has become nothing but a myth. Everyone forgot what it looked like, how it was used, and what happened to it. Only a few retained echoes of its power, but even these memories are starting to fade. Those who dare seek answers wander through the dungeons of ancient temples, where darkness has swallowed even the oldest traces.

But in the darkest corners of the world, in the depths of ruined sanctuaries, something is hidden that could restore magic ‚Äî or become its final, destructive surge, capable of forever changing the fate of the world.
